# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of level of experience, age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and considerate  
- Providing constructive feedback  
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind  
- Toxic, aggressive, or insulting comments  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission  
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate  

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in response to any violation, including:
- Temporary or permanent bans  
- Removal of contributions  
- Blocking of GitHub accounts on this repository  

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:
- GitHub issues  
- Pull requests  
- Discussions  
- Any other project communication channels  

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the repository owner directly.

All complaints will be reviewed fairly and without prejudice.
